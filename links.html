General Information

What: This one-day workshop on analyzing next-generation sequencing data (NGS) will cover the following topics:

  • cloud computing for analyzing NGS;
  • the basics of mapping, variant calling, and assembly; and
  • mRNAseq quantification and differential expression analysis.

The workshop will also introduce complete cloud-based workflows for de novo mRNAseq and metagenome sequence analysis. Attendees will also be introduced to a much large set of NGS materials available online at http://ged.msu.edu/angus/tutorials-2013/. No prior computational experience is required; some prior biological expertise is useful.

Who: The course is aimed at postgraduate students and other scientists who are familiar with basic concepts in biology. Note that the workshop is open to everyone, not just PyCon attendees.
